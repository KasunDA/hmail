== HMail

A responsive mobile-friendly website in ruby on rails and backbone.js that offers children an alternate way to use gmail such that parents can monitor the child's email activity and  set up a whitelist of email contacts.  Children are completely isolated from spam and cannot send to or receive from contacts who are not on the whitelist.

* Responsive
* Mobile-Friendly
* Written in Ruby on Rails and Backbone.js
* Deployable to Heroku
